Vince Staples Fresh From The West Coast

Photo by Tobi Nielsen via Wikimedia Commons.

Vince Staples is an explosive West Coast rapper from North Long Beach, California. Staple's emerged over seven years ago creating a different kind of rap that he explicitly says is NOT gangster rap. Many of his songs and music videos are politically charged and focus on bringing about social justice for the lower class and minorities of America. Staples music is an honest reality check that makes his listeners think about what is happening in our country. His fast, upbeat rapping style is captivating, and would not be possible without his incredible breath support.
